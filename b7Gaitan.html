What is Biology?

According to Brittanica, Biology is a branch of science that deals with living organisms and their vital processes. Biology encompasses diverse fields, including botany, conservation, ecology, evolution, genetics, marine biology, medicine, microbiology, molecular biology, physiology, and zoology among others. Although Biology has many branches, they are still interrelated.

What is Zoology?

Zoology, also known as Animal Biology, is the study of animals. Its studies include the structure, embryology, classification, habits, and distribution of all animals, both living and extinct, and how they interact with their ecosystems. A Zoologist is a person who studies animals and engages in the field of Zoology. I love Zoology because I love animals, especially Cats.

What is a Cat and Why do I like them?

Cats (Felis catus) are the only domesticated species in the family Felidae. Cats have a coat of fur which can be different colors and they also have retractable claws. Cats also have excellent night vision and love to hunt small rodents such as mice during the night. I love cats because they are cute and cuddly.
